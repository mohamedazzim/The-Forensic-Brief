## What It Is

The Decision Envelope is a structured template for documenting any decision made with AI assistance. It captures the context, inputs, AI recommendations, human judgment, and final rationale — creating an auditable record.

## When to Use

Use the Decision Envelope whenever:
- An AI system recommends a decision that affects people
- Regulatory compliance requires decision documentation
- You need to demonstrate meaningful human oversight
- Incident investigation requires understanding past decisions

## How to Use

1. Fill in the context section before making the decision
2. Document the AI's recommendation and confidence level
3. Record your assessment and any overrides
4. Complete the rationale section with your reasoning
5. Store the completed envelope per your retention policy

## Inline Preview

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Decision ID | Unique identifier | Yes |
| Date/Time | When the decision was made | Yes |
| Decision Maker | Name and role | Yes |
| AI System | Which AI was involved | Yes |
| AI Recommendation | What the AI suggested | Yes |
| AI Confidence | System's confidence level | Yes |
| Human Assessment | Your evaluation of the AI's recommendation | Yes |
| Decision Made | The actual decision | Yes |
| Rationale | Why this decision was made | Yes |
| Override Reason | If different from AI recommendation, why | Conditional |
| Review Date | When this decision should be reviewed | Optional |

## About This Book

This book examines the fundamental failure of human-in-the-loop governance in AI systems. Drawing on incident analysis, regulatory research, and case studies, it demonstrates that inserting humans into automated workflows does not constitute oversight unless specific conditions are met.

The book corresponds to the "Human in Control" essay series on The Forensic Brief.

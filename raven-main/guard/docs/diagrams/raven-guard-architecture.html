Incident SLA + Escalation

Escalation ladder · SLA enforced
P1 Critical
Production down · data loss risk
SLA: 15 minutes
→ Escalation contact SMS
→ Prism7 CRITICAL flag
→ Auto-rollback if possible
P2 High
Degraded · potential breach
SLA: 1 hour
→ Prism7 HIGH flag
→ Team lead email
→ Approval flow started
P3 Medium
Anomaly · policy violation
SLA: 24 hours
→ Prism7 daily digest
→ Logged for review
